Our Magical Story

It started with a copper lamp found in a dusty Florentine attic. Legend says that while the genie stayed in the lamp, the secret recipes for the world's most ethereal frozen treats were hidden in its carvings.

At Gelato Genie, we don't just churn cream; we weave stories. Each batch is inspired by moments of pure joy—the first day of spring, a forgotten love letter, or a midnight wish upon a shooting star.
